Hi
I'm Aimee

I’m a Multimedia Graphic Designer based in Colorado Springs, CO. I specialize in all things creative, fun, and colorful—and I’m always eager to learn something new! I like to think of myself as a “perfectionist who knows when to stop.” When you work with me, I’ll always strive to bring your vision to life as authentically as you imagine it, without wasting your time. I’ve always had a passion for creativity, especially when it comes to designing posters, stickers, branding, advertising, and other unique pieces.

What Drives Me?

When it comes to design, what drives me is the impact good design can have on a community. Whether it’s helping a small business grow, shining a light on valuable local resources, or informing people about the options available to them, it all comes together to make lives better. I believe anything worthwhile is created in the best possible environment—that’s why I strive to make the design process fun, easy, collaborative, and thoughtful.

How Did I Get Into Design?

Ever since I was a kid, my mom and eldest sisters encouraged creativity. I’d sit for hours drawing, painting, doodling, making arts and crafts—just being creative with them—and I absolutely loved it! It felt like my calling, my safe space, and the thing I was most passionate about. I could get completely lost in it. As I got older and started thinking about college and my future career, all I knew was that I wanted to be happy, and being creative made me happy. The only problem was, I didn’t know exactly what part of the creative industry I wanted to pursue. I never understood why I found things like advertisements, editing, layout, type, and fonts just as fascinating and creative as a painting. That curiosity stuck with me. When it came time to apply for college, I knew I didn’t want to be a fine artist, I didn’t draw consistantly enough, and my interest in fine art was more about admiring others’ work rather than creating it. So, I scheduled an advising appointment to explore the creative class options. That’s when my advisor told me she thought I’d be a great fit for the Multimedia Graphic Design program at Pikes Peak State College. Within the first two weeks of classes, I was hooked. I was having so much fun trying something new, even though it was a challenge at first, and from there, the rest is history!
